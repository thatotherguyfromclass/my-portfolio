Krusty Krab

Welcome to the Krusty Krab, the heart and soul of Bikini Bottom's fast food scene and the proud home of the world-famous Krabby Patty. From the moment you step inside our legendary underwater eatery, you're not just a customer — you're family. Born from humble beginnings inside an old lobster trap, the Krusty Krab has grown into a true icon, known across the ocean floor for its irresistible burgers, loyal crew, and unforgettable atmosphere. We believe in more than just filling stomachs — we believe in filling hearts. Every Krabby Patty that slides off the grill carries a little piece of the ocean's best: carefully stacked ingredients, toasted buns, melty cheese, crispy lettuce, and that mysteriously delicious patty that has baffled Plankton for years.
